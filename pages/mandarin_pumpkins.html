Spooky Spider Eggs

This is a cute way to serve the kids fruit during the Halloween season.

The recipe involves turning plain Mandarins into cute miniature pumpkins!

Ingredients

  • 10 Mandarin or Clementine Oranges, Peeled, Pit Removed
  • 2 Stalks of Celery

Steps

  1. Cut celery into small pieces
  2. Stick a piece of celery into the middle of each peeled mandarin orange
